How do I report a critical bug?
Critical bugs should be reported using the "Report a Bug" option. Please include steps to reproduce, screenshots if available, and mark it as critical in your description.
What is the response time for inquiries?
We typically respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours. Critical bug reports are prioritized and usually addressed within 24 hours.
Can I track the status of my bug report?
Yes, all submitted bug reports receive a unique tracking ID. You can check the status in your account dashboard under "My Reports".
How can I join your beta testing program?
Beta testing opportunities are announced in our Discord community and through email newsletters. Make sure your notification preferences are enabled in your account settings.
